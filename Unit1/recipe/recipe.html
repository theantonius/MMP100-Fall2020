How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • 1 tablespoon of Peanut Butter
  • 1 tablespoon of Jam

Directions

  1. Take one piece of bread and spread about a tablespoon of peanut butter on it.
  2. Take another piece of bread and spread about a tablespoon of jam on it.
  3. Combine the two slices: Place your peanut butter slice on top of your jam slice of bread, making sure the peanut butter and the jam meet. Try to match them up as evenly as possible. Enjoy!
